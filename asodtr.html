IBM ThinkPad T40

Overview

The T40 was known for its robust build quality and excellent keyboard. It was a favorite among business professionals.

Pros:
  • Durable design
  • Excellent keyboard feel
  • Good battery life
  • Lightweight for its class
Cons:
  • Limited graphics performance
  • Average display quality

IBM ThinkPad T41

Overview

The T41 improved upon the T40 with better graphics options and a more powerful processor.

Pros:
  • Improved graphics performance
  • Solid build quality
  • Good battery life
Cons:
  • Heavier than T40
  • Pricey for the specs

IBM ThinkPad T42

Overview

The T42 was praised for its excellent display and overall reliability, making it a popular choice.

Pros:
  • Great display quality
  • Reliable performance
Cons:
  • Higher price point
  • Limited upgrade options

IBM ThinkPad T43

Overview

The T43 offered fast performance and excellent customer support, making it a solid investment.

Pros:
  • Fast performance
  • Excellent customer support
Cons:
  • Can run hot under load
  • Heavier than previous models
