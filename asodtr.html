IBM ThinkPad Reviews (2000-2005)

T40 T41 T42 T43

IBM ThinkPad T40

Overview

The T40 was known for its robust build quality and excellent keyboard. It was a favorite among business professionals.

Pros:
  • Durable design
  • Excellent keyboard feel
  • Good battery life
  • Lightweight for its class
Cons:
  • Limited graphics performance
  • Average display quality

IBM ThinkPad T41

Overview

The T41 improved upon the T40 with better graphics options and a more powerful processor.

Pros:
  • Improved graphics performance
  • Solid build quality
  • Good battery life
  • Comfortable keyboard
Cons:
  • Heavier than the T40
  • Pricey compared to competitors

IBM ThinkPad T42

Overview

The T42 was a refinement of the T41, offering better performance and enhanced features.

Pros:
  • Excellent performance
  • Great battery life
  • High-quality display
  • Robust security features
Cons:
  • Can run hot under load
  • Limited upgrade options

IBM ThinkPad T43

Overview

The T43 was one of the last models in the T series before Lenovo took over, known for its reliability and performance.

Pros:
  • Strong performance
  • Excellent keyboard
  • Good battery life
  • Durable chassis
Cons:
  • Heavier than some competitors
  • Graphics performance could be better
